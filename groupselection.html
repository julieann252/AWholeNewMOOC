Join a Course Group

What are groups?

  • Students who join course groups are 75% more likely to complete their course.
  • When you join a group, you will be placed on a team of students and work with them to answer discussion questions and complete projects.
  • You can either join a group with your friends or be placed into a group based on your meeting preferences and location.
  • Group sizes can range from 4 people to 10 people. If you don't have enough friends for a complete group, you can join another partial group.
  • You should expect an additional time commitment of 2-3 hours per week for group work.
