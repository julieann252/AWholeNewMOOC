Join a Course Group

What are groups?

If you join a group, you will be placed on a team of five other students and work with them to answer discussion questions and complete projects. You can either join a group with your friends or be placed into a group based on your meeting preferences and location. You should expect an additional time commitment of 2-3 hours per week for group work.

Students who join course groups are 75% more likely to complete their course.
